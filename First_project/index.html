Super Mario Bros 3

Mario Bros 3 is one of the numerous retro games that earned its place in the hearts of many (including me). this is a game made by nintendo and the fourth game of the Mario series released by the director Shigeru Miyamoto in october 23rd of 1988. This game was Published for the NES console.

What is this game about?

This is a 2D platform game about a plumber who has to rescue a princess (the Princess Toadstool) from a dragon (Bowser). he has to go through 8 different worlds, defeating different enemies, going through different levels and castles, and using exciting power-ups that make the game so much more fun. There is also a 2 player mode, but it is not like the ones we have today where both players can play at the same time, it is more like a "wait for your turn" type of thing, because after you clear one level, it's the next player's turn to play. Despite that, I strongly recommend this game to anyone.
